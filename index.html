About Letter Left Behind

Letter Left Behind is a unique outdoor treasure hunt experience in London that blends time-travel storytelling with real-world exploration. Walk through Waterloo, Trafalgar Square, Charing Cross, and other iconic spots while solving riddles and uncovering a personalised letter from the past. A memorable gift and activity for locals and visitors alike.

How It Works

Master Archibald guides you through 10 locations across central London. At each stop, solve clues and answer questions based on what you discover. As you progress, you'll uncover pieces of a letter left behind from 1800—a message that has traveled through time to reach you.

Perfect for: Couples, friends, families, or anyone who loves adventure, history, and mystery. Play solo or as a group!

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the treasure hunt take?
Most players complete the journey in 2–3 hours. You can take breaks and go at your own pace.
Do I need to book in advance?
Yes. Book online to receive your game link. The game is available 15 minutes before your chosen slot.
Can I play with a group?
Absolutely! The game is designed for solo players, couples, families, or groups. Everyone uses the same link to play together.
Is it suitable for children?
Yes. The puzzles and historical content are family-friendly. Younger children may need adult help with some clues.
Can I give this as a gift?
Yes! Add a personalised message that will be revealed at the end of the game. Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, or surprises.
